# China Follow-up Research Plan

## Main Question

Can China be added to the dashboard comparison pipeline using an official or regulator-backed electricity price source without inventing a weak methodology?

## Subtopics

1. National retail tariff source
   - Search for official national household electricity tariff datasets or notices with stable publication and interpretable units.

2. Wholesale / spot market source
   - Search for official national or province-level spot market price publications with stable access.

3. Defensible proxy path
   - If national data is not available, determine whether a province-level or statistical proxy is strong enough for this repo and what caveats it would impose.

## Expected Output

1. A yes/no answer on whether China can be added now.
2. If yes, the exact source and methodology.
3. If no, the narrowest acceptable proxy and the caveats required.
